ANSEBA REGION

nseba Region is one of the six administrative regions, located in northern Eritrea. The capital and the most populous city in the region is Keren. The region is named after Anseba River, which passed through most parts of the region. The average elevation in the region is around 1,800 m to 2,100 m.

Sub Zones

  2. Ali Gidir
  3. Hamelmalo
  4. Adi Tekeleza
  5. Tinqulehas
