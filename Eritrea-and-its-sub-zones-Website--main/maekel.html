MAEKEL REGION(CENTRAL)

Central Region (Italian: Regione Centrale), also known as the Ma'ekel or Maakel Region, is an administrative region (or zoba) in central Eritrea.[2] The region was formed on 15 April 1996, from the historical province of Hamasien. The region is located on the central plateau, and sits at an average of about 2,250 metres (7,380 ft) above sea level. It contains Asmara, the capital and largest city of Eritrea.[3]
