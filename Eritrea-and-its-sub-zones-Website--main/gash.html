GASH BARKA REGION

Gash-Barka (Tigrinya: ጋሽ-ባርካ, Italian: Regione di Gasc-Barca) is one of the six regions of Eritrea. ... The total area of the province was 33,200 km2 and the density was 21.35 persons per km2., making up roughly one-third of Eritrea. The region is dubbed as the "breadbasket" of the country as it is rich in agriculture
